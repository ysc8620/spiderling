Product details of SONY STR-DN1060 AV Receiver with Music Services

Breath-taking Audio, Stunning Visual Immersion Elevate your entertainment experience with Sony's STR-DN1060 High-Resolution Audio AV Receiver. Built with incredible 7.2ch surround sound at 165W per channel*, fill your room with rich audio quality no matter what you're listening to. Also, with 4K upscaling and pass through, every motion picture you indulge in becomes more expressive and life-like than ever.  

Music As The Artist Intended

Experience digital music to the fullest with High-Resolution Audio. By capturing music at a higher rate than CD, increasing both audio samples per second and the bit rate accuracy of each sample, High-Resolution Audio offers an uncompromised reproduction of an original recording.

Sound Enhanced

Upscale your compressed digital files (MP3, AAC and WMA) 
to near High-Resolution Audio quality. Sonys Digital Sound Enhancement Engine HX (DSEE HX) restores high-range sound lost in the compression process and reproduces your music in rich sound, closer to the original recording quality.


Fill Your Home with Music
Let music flow throughout your home with STR-DN1060's wireless Multi-room feature, using the SongPal Link. Control the volume, sound field and each room's choice of music easily using the SongPal app that lets you link up other Sony soundbars and speakers around your home. With Google Cast built-in, you can stream your favourite songs from your personal devices to your sound systems.

High Quality Wireless Listening

Enjoy above CD quality wireless audio via Bluetooth with Sonys newly-developed LDAC technology that enables reproduction of 3x more data compared to compressed audio files providing an enhanced wireless listening experience like never before. With 3x more data transmitted compared to normal Bluetooth* technology, LDAC can maintain the original bit depth and frequency of 24bit/96kHz, thus giving you a fuller, richer High-Resolution Audio enjoyment.

Thanks to LDAC, get connected to the STR-DN1060 using other compatible Sony LDAC headphones, to begin your pristine, wireless listening experience.
